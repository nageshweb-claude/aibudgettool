What is Perplexity AI? The AI Search Engine

June 2026 · 4 min read

Perplexity is an AI search engine that gives you answers with citations — like a research assistant that shows its work.

What is Perplexity?

Perplexity is an AI-powered search engine. Instead of links, it gives direct answers with numbered citations to original sources. Think of it as Google meets ChatGPT — but with verifiable sources.

Why Use Over Google?

Google gives links to click. Perplexity gives answers with sources. For research — fact-checking, product comparison, academic topics, current events — Perplexity saves enormous time.

Free vs Pro

Free tier is generous. Pro ($20/month) gives unlimited deep searches with more powerful models.

Best Uses

Research, fact-checking, product comparisons, market research, academic research, current events analysis. For creative writing or coding, use ChatGPT or Claude instead.
