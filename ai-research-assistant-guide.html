Using AI as a Research Assistant: Best Practices

June 2026 · 3 min read

AI research assistants speed up literature review and synthesis, but require careful fact-checking.

Start with Perplexity

For any factual research, Perplexity's cited answers give you a verified starting point with sources you can click through and verify.

Use Claude for Synthesis

Paste multiple sources into Claude (large context window) and ask it to synthesize themes, contradictions, and gaps across all of them at once.

Use ChatGPT for Brainstorming

Generate research questions, alternative angles, and potential counterarguments before you commit to a thesis.

Always Verify

Never cite an AI-generated fact without checking the primary source. AI can hallucinate citations that look completely real.
