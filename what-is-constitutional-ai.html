What is Constitutional AI? Anthropic's Safety Approach

June 2026 · 3 min read

Constitutional AI trains models to critique and revise their own outputs against a set of guiding principles.

How It Works

Instead of human labelers scoring every output (RLHF), the AI is given a set of principles (a 'constitution') and learns to critique and revise its own outputs against those principles.

Advantage Over RLHF

Scales better (less human labor), more consistent (principles do not vary like human opinions), and more transparent (the principles are explicit and auditable).
