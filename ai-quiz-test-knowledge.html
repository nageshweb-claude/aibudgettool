How Scoring Works

Each round picks 10 random questions from your chosen category, so the quiz is different every time. Every correct answer earns 10 points (100 max per round). At the end, you're graded: Beginner (0-30), Intermediate (40-50), Advanced (60-70), or Expert (80-100). Get 80+ and confetti rains down.

Your scores are saved to the leaderboard — compete with yourself across categories, or share with friends to see who knows AI better.

After the Quiz: Flashcard Review

The real learning happens in the review. After your results, click "Review Answers" to see flashcards of every question — your answer, the correct answer, and a detailed explanation. This spaced repetition approach helps concepts stick in long-term memory.
