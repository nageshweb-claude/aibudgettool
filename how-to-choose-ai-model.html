How to Choose the Right AI Model for Your Task

June 2026 · 4 min read

Match your task to the right AI: Claude for code, Gemini for research, ChatGPT for broad daily use.

Start with the Task, Not the Brand

Coding and technical writing: Claude. Broad daily tasks, images, voice: ChatGPT. Research and Google integration: Gemini. Sourced research with citations: Perplexity. Self-hosting or maximum cost control: Llama or DeepSeek.

Consider Cost at Scale

For high-volume API use, Gemini Flash or DeepSeek Flash dramatically reduce costs compared to flagship models, often with acceptable quality for simpler tasks.

Consider Context Needs

Processing entire codebases or books favors models with the largest context windows, currently led by Llama 4 Scout at 10 million tokens.

Test Before Committing

Run the same real task through 2-3 models before deciding. The 'best' model varies significantly by specific use case.
