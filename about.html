About AI Budget Tool

A free, fast, and private budget calculator built for everyone — no sign-up, no tracking, just clarity.

Our Mission

AI Budget Tool was built with one simple goal — to give everyone, anywhere in the world, a fast and free way to understand their personal finances. Whether you're a student in India, a freelancer in Europe, or a professional in the US, managing your money should be simple and free.

Most budgeting apps require you to sign up, connect your bank account, or pay a subscription. We believe that's unnecessary for the basics. Enter your income, add your expenses, get your numbers — instantly, privately, without giving away your data.

We support 50+ global currencies so this tool works for everyone, not just users in one country. Our savings analysis gives you smart, actionable feedback based on globally recognised personal finance benchmarks.
